About the founder

About Stephen Simon

As the team leader of Clan of Kings and director of Young Lead Firm, Stephen Simon is a renowned kingmaker, prophet with an apostolic mandate, and dedicated equipper. With a passion for cultivating visionary leaders and influential thinkers, his vision is to guide individuals in uncovering their purpose and identity, while creating effective frameworks that support their growth and impact with a kingdom mindset.

My Story

My passion and purpose were derived from the experiences I had growing up, where I saw people living average lives, lacking a sense of purpose, identity, or direction. Everyone seemed consumed by the daily grind, rather than striving to live and lead significant lives. This burden weighed heavily on me, aligning with God's calling into ministry.

My Mission

There is no greater mission for me. There is no higher goal than to help you realize your significance and potential. That's why I've dedicated my life to what I believe I do best: Teach, Equip, and Impart!

Who We Are

We are a dynamic and motivated team of individuals who are passionate about making a positive impact in our community and world. We are committed to personal growth, leadership development, and creating positive change through our actions and initiatives.

Vision

To empower and inspire the young generation of leaders to make a positive impact in their community and world, through mentorship, education, and opportunities for growth through our actions and initiatives.
